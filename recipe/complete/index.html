INGREDIENTS

PER SERVING


1 instant cup noodle
200g white rice
2 eggs
2 tbsp neutral oil
1 tbsp sesame oil
1 tbsp sesame seeds

EQIPMENTS

MUST HAVE


Pot
Plastic bag
Frying pan
Tablespoon
Stirrer
Plate

INSTRUCTIONS

  • Step 1

    Open the cup noodle and take the soup base out.

  • Step 2

    Transfer all remaining contents to a zip-lock bag and crush them into small pieces.

  • Step 3

    Pour the crushed ingredients back into the cup, and pour the soup base.

  • Step 4

    Pour boiling water just to cover the noodles, and leave it for 2 minutes.

  • Step 5

    Heat a pan on medium heat. Add 3 tbsp of oil once the pan comes up to temperature.

  • Step 6

    Crack the an egg into the pan and scramble it.

  • Step 7

    Add rice into the pan and mix with the scrambled egg.

  • Step 8

    Stir the cooked noodle, then pour it into the pan. Stir-fry all the ingredients for 4 minutes.

  • Step 9

    Add 1 tbsp of sesame oil to the noodle cup, and sprinkle ½ tbsp of sesame seeds.

  • Step 10

    Transfer the stir-fried rice back to the noodle cup. Press the rice firmly with spoon so that it holds its shape.

  • Step 11

    Place a plate on top of the cup and flip the plate and cup at the same time. Slowly remove the cup to reveal the fried rice.

  • Step 12

    Fry an egg until your desired consistency, place it on top, and enjoy!
